## Vision coherence — Keep

- **Mythological brand holds.** The vision doc's "painterly mythology-faithful" frame is intact. Anansi's signature gold carries through the HPBar accent, ability radial, ultimate radial glow, forward indicator cone on the placeholder capsule. The character's silhouette reads "something trickster-weaver-like" even at capsule fidelity — the forward cone gives an unambiguous "this end is the front" tell that isn't fighting the player's intuition.
- **ADR discipline is exemplary.** Every non-trivial design decision in Phase 2 is anchored in an ADR (0008 HP tiers, 0010 AI clone, 0012 Sacred Grove overcast, 0013 particle choice). T-100's CB writeup cross-references them all; no ADR was quietly re-opened.
- **Accessibility is not an afterthought.** HUD HPBar pairs color with a ▼ glyph at <30% HP. Ultimate radial pulse is suppressed under reducedMotion. Ability + ultimate carry keymap hints. Score dots in ScoreBar use signature-color + position (left/right) so the colorblind pairing is baked. Every UI primitive (T-005) honored the three a11y settings without a retrofit pass.
- **Determinism commitment is real.** ADR-0006 flows through the entire stack: math utils have no Math.random, scheduler is tick-keyed, RNG is seeded, bot AI's 120-tick replay test passes byte-identically. This is the most important load-bearing commitment for the v1.1 netcode and it's in place.
- **Bundle discipline.** 4.24 MB precache vs 25 MB budget is 6× headroom. We haven't blown the budget chasing visual fidelity. The hand-rolled VFX choice (ADR-0013 over three-nebula) paid for itself — +11 kB gzip for the whole particle system.

## Vision coherence — Adjust

Phase 3 rigging (T-101 deferred) or a Phase-3.5 Path-C art pass is load-bearing for a credible "vertical slice" claim to anyone outside the team. **Action for Phase 3**: if Path B, rigging lands in parallel with Brigid authoring. If Path C, rigging is the single highest-ROI polish task.

For the automated QA this is invisible; for a user hitting the live URL, the first impression is "did my browser mute?". **Action for Phase 3**: source even one placeholder CC0 ambient loop + one button-click SFX + one hit SFX to break the silence; the rest of the mix can stay sparse. 15 minutes of Freesound.org from a user-supplied URL set.

The bot is behind the player at match start. **Action**: rotate the default camera 180° OR spawn both Anansis at perpendicular quadrants so both are visible from the player's spawn orientation.
