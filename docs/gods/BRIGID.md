## At-a-glance

- **Mythology**: Celtic / Gaelic (Irish)
- **Role**: Area-denial mage, zone-control, anchor
- **Signature color**: Ember orange (#E8662A)
- **Basic**: Ember Brand (firebolt, 95 dmg, 0.55 s fire rate, burn patch)
- **Signature**: Hearthstone (totem heal/burn, 10 s CD, 150 HP destructible)
- **Ultimate**: Forge's Breath (expanding line of fire, 1200 charge, 0.7 s cast)
- **Passive**: Smith's Patience (basic-attack burn DoT stacks up to 3x per target)

## Cultural source notes

Brigid is drawn from pre-Christian Celtic tradition — goddess of smithcraft, healing, and poetry. Primary sources for research: *Lebor Gabála Érenn* (public domain), James MacKillop's *A Dictionary of Celtic Mythology*. Avoid conflation with the Christianized Saint Brigid; v1 Brigid is the pre-Christian triadic goddess. Visual design emphasizes the forge/hearth aspect (the most play-relevant of her three triadic domains).
