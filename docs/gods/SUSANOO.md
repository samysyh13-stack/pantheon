## At-a-glance

- **Mythology**: Japanese / Shinto
- **Role**: Close-range brawler, dive, burst
- **Signature color**: Storm cyan (#2E9FB5)
- **Basic**: Kusanagi Cut (2 m arc, 110 dmg, 0.65 s fire rate; 3rd hit lunges)
- **Signature**: Storm Step (6 m dash, 7 s CD, static trail, +50% next attack)
- **Ultimate**: Orochi's Wake (1 s channel → 8-head 360° sweep, 1400 charge, 60 dmg per head)
- **Passive**: Storm's Rebirth (on kill: +40 HP, -2 s Storm Step CD)

## Open questions

- 8-head tracking priority when 2+ enemies in range (same target or split?)

## Cultural source notes

Susanoo-no-Mikoto is the storm god of the Shinto pantheon, brother to Amaterasu. Primary sources: the *Kojiki* (Basil Hall Chamberlain trans., public domain) and the *Nihon Shoki* (W.G. Aston trans., public domain). The Yamata-no-Orochi (eight-headed serpent) myth is the specific narrative hook for the ultimate — Susanoo's slaying of the serpent and recovery of the Kusanagi blade from its tail. Design must honor the "banished god" arc — Susanoo is turbulent, not merely aggressive; his kit expresses storm-god temperament.
